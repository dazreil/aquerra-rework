Aquerra Lab

The jet inventory is the “Jet stock” panel inside the game canvas, on the right of the grid. Click a stock type to choose a pump. Left-click a wall/water edge to place it. Left-click a pump to delete it and start recharge. Right-click a pump to restart it. Right-click or shift-click water to move the tuber. Alt-click water to move the goal.
